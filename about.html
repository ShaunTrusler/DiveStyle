WHY WE WANT YOU TO LIVE THE SCUBALIFE

ScubaLife scuba traing facility was founded and created with the unique lifestyle offing that Scuba Diving has.
The world has so much more to offer when you take a step off the beaten path and see what else is on offer.

When you become a Scuba Diver, you will have the opportunity to experience the Land based side as well as the ocean side of this world and all the wonders that fill its unique offering
As a Scuba Diver you will be able to explore the Famous 'Great Barrier Reef' off the coast of Australia or dive the 'THISTELGORM', a WWI cargo ship that sank off the coast of Egypt. You will be able to experience the underwater world filled with so many bustling ecosystems and fish life that you will not know where to look. You will get see that the ocean is not a fearful place, but a vibrant system of life and colours.

ScubaLife instructors are PADI Certified and we all have a passion for teaching and showing you and all of your friends and family this new world that awaits. Change your fun LIFESTYLE into an awesome DIVESTLE and live more.
